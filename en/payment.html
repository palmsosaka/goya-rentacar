Important Notes

Credit Card Recommendation

We recommend paying by credit card for convenience. Foreign-issued credit cards are accepted as long as they are Visa, Mastercard, JCB, American Express, or Diners Club.

What's Included in the Price

Your total includes the base rental fee, insurance plan, car navigation, and ETC device. We show you the total amount upfront — no hidden fees.
